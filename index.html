DITU Help Crunch, the Socio-Academic Portal of DITU, is the most frequently visited website of DITU. We aim to make the life of every DITian simpler & turn these four years into a smoother ride!

Services

The application will contain certain features which will be going to benefit a student in many aspects.

Query

If you have any problem, then you can ask and then anyone can answer that question whether it is related to studies or placements. And we can also search any query in it from the earlier solved queries.

Question Papers

You will be getting all the previous year question papers by just one click.

Book Sharing

Anyone can put his book here who is willing to share or sell and the one who want the available book can contact with the sharer or seller.

News Update

All news and upcoming events related to university and university’s club can be found here.

Notes Section

You will get the notes of all the subjects available, and anyone who is willing can also post it.

Job Openings

You can get the contacts of our alumni who are currently working in big MNCs. Students will get all the information regarding new job opportunities.

Faculty Contacts

All the university’s professors and assistant professors contact details will be provided here.

Cab Sharing

Anyone who is going outside the campus and booking cab can put his ride details so that others who also want to go at the same destination can contact that person.
